DEMON SLAYER: KIMETSU NO YAIBA ASAKUSA ARC


Tanjiro and Nezuko cross paths with two powerful demons who fight with magical weapons. Even help from Tamayo and Yushiro may not be enough to defeat these demons who claim to belong to the Twelve Kizuki that directly serve Muzan, the demon responsible for all of Tanjiro's woes! But if these demons can be defeated, what secrets can they reveal about Muzan.


In this arc you will meet Tamayo and Yushiro, two rare, docile and good-natured demons who live side by side with humans. However, the debut of these two characters is not the only interesting thing here, in this part Tanjiro finally meets Muzan Kibutsuji which makes him quite shaken. This arc tells the story of when Tanjiro meets Tamayo and Yushiro who take him into their territory to hear their idea about an antidote for demons. However, his visit then invited two demon candidates for the Twelve Kizuki, Susamaru and Yahaba who served Muzan.
