DEMON SLAYER: KIMETSU NO YAIBA SIBLING'S BOND ARC


Tanjiro kamado is a kind-hearted and intelligent boy who lives with his family in the mountains. He became his family's breadwinner after his father's death, traveling to the nearby village to sell charcoal. Everything changed when he came home one day to discover his family was attacked and slaughtered by a demon. Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko were the sole survivors of the incident, with Nezuko being transformed into a demon, but still surprisingly showing signs of human emotion and thought.


The first sequence in the Demon Slayer arc is Final Selection. This arc tells the story of the origin of the character Tanjiro, who is a charcoal seller. He then decided to become a demon hunter after seeing his family slaughtered by demons. Apart from that, this arc also introduces other characters such as Nezuko and Zenitsu. In this section, it is told that Tanjiro received several years of training from Urokodaki, then Tanjiro went to the Final Selection for the entrance exam for candidates for the Demon Slayer Corps. In this selection, they will pass if they manage to get out of the demon-infested mountain alive for seven days. In this arc you are also introduced to most of the main characters, namely Tanjiro, Nezuko, Zenitsu, and introduced the Hashira concept with Giyu Tomioka.
