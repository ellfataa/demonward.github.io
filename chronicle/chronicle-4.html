DEMON SLAYER: KIMETSU NO YAIBA MUGEN TRAIN ARC


Furthermore, after the first season ended, Kimetsu no Yaiba - The Movie: Mugen Train was released, the first anime film from Kimetsu no Yaiba which was a sequel to season one. The film, which was released in October 2020, has a duration of 116 minutes. The story focuses on Tanjiro, Nezuko, Zenitsu, and Inosuke who get a mission with Kyojurou Rengoku's Pillar of Fire in Mugen Train, where the devil who is the main antagonist is one Upper Moon.


This arc was adapted into a film, where the main focus is on the attention-stealing actions of Hashira Rengoku. The trio of Tanjiro, Zenitsu, and Inosuke were ordered to accompany Rengoku to defeat Enmu, a Low Rank demon who unites his soul in a train that can put anyone who rides the train in a dreamland. Even all the demon slayers entered the dream world so that they were unconscious. However, the demon slayers managed to come to their senses and take the fight to Enmu and succeeded in eradicating him. However, at the end of the story, Akaza, the High Level Demon, appears and makes Rengoku fight to the death and takes the life of this fire hashira.
