DEMON SLAYER: KIMETSU NO YAIBA MOUNT NATAGUMO ARC


At Mount Natagumo, Tanjiro, Zenitsu and Inosuke battle a terrible family of spider demons. Taking on such powerful enemies demands all the skill and luck Tanjiro has as he and his companions fight to rescue Nezuko from the spiders' web. The battle is drawing in other Demon Slayers but not all of them will leave Mt. Natagumo alive...or in one piece.


This time Tanjiro, Zenitsu, Inosuke climb Mount Natagumo in their first group mission to defeat a family of terrible demon spiders. After carrying out a dangerous mission against former members of the 12 Kizuki, Tanjiro, Zenitsu and Inosuke finally meet the first member of the 12 Kizuki named Rui. Rui is the first high-level demon introduced in the story. As this arc progresses, Rui is the strongest demon Tanjiro has ever encountered. Apart from introducing the members of the 12 Kizuki and the powers they have, this arc also reveals that Tanjiro can use sun breath, aka Hinokami Kagura. In this arc we also meet two hashira or demon hunting pillars, Giyu Tomioka and also Shinobi, a hashira with insect breathing.
