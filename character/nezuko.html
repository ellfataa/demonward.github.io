NEZUKO KAMADO

Nezuko is one of the youngest characters in Demon Slayer. He is only 12 years old at the start of the story when he turns into a demon. A few episodes later, he turns 14 years old. But, physically, he didn't grow much after that tragic day. That's something that won't change until Tanjiro finds the antidote.
Her transformation into a demon naturally changed Nezuko's personality. He became braver and more outspoken than when he was still human. However, a number of its good qualities still persist.
