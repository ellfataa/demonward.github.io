KIBUTSUJI MUZAN

The antagonist in Demon Slayer is one of the most interesting characters in the entire series. Muzan may not appear much in this anime. But, his cold attitude like when he destroyed most of his powerful lieutenants was quite terrifying to see.
Muzan's strength seems to be very great. No one really understands how Tanjiro and the other Demon Slayer members have a chance against this powerful creature. But, there's still plenty of time for them to get stronger and give Muzan a good fight.
