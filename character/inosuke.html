INOSUKE HASIBARA

Inosuke usually enters a fight without thinking. Even though he is very capable of taking care of himself, his habit of overestimating his own strengths usually gets him into trouble. In particular, because he always wanted to challenge anyone who could possibly surpass him in combat.
Inosuke raised wild boars. This is reflected in the choice of mask he wears. This background suggests that he is fighting against human traditions, emotions and customs. However, thanks to his affiliation with the Demon Slayer Corps, slowly, he began to change.
