UZUI TENGEN

Tengen Uzui is a Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba character who is the main supporting character and also the main character in Arc Entertainment District. This male character is 198 cm tall and was born on October 31.
Tengen is a former member of the Demon Slayer Corps, who previously served as the Hashira of Sound. Sound Breathing is a breathing style that Tengen himself created. Sound Breathing appears to combine Tengen's enhanced sense of hearing, the Musical Score technique, as well as his shinobi skills in wielding the dual machete Nichirin and his anti-demon bomb.
