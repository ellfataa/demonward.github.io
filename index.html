Chronicle

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. The story tells of Tanjiro Kamado, a boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family was massacred by demons and his younger sister, Nezuko, was turned into a demon.

This manga was first released in Weekly Shonen Jump magazine in February 2016. It tells the story of a young man named Tanjiro Kamado who lived in Japan during the Edo Period.
